## 6. Core Services

### 6.1 Test Preparation
- In-house IELTS coaching (5-week classroom programs)
- In-house PTE coaching
- Fateh originated as an IELTS training institute and received the "Award of Excellence" Extra Learn from the University of Cambridge ESOL Examinations

### 6.2 Admissions Consulting
- Course and university shortlisting based on student profile
- Application processing and submission
- Statement of Purpose (SOP) editing and review
- Offer letter follow-up and negotiation

### 6.3 Visa Support
- End-to-end visa documentation
- Mock visa interviews
- Unique "in-house VFS" option in select offices — students can file visas directly at the Fateh office
- 99%+ visa success rate across UK and Ireland

### 6.4 Financial Guidance
- Education loan assistance
- Scholarship identification and application support
- Strategic investment in Reeudo (cross-border student payment fintech) to support seamless tuition fee transfers

### 6.5 Pre-Departure & Post-Landing Support
- Pre-departure orientation sessions ("Meet & Greet" events held across 7 cities)
- Health insurance guidance
- Post-landing support and student community
